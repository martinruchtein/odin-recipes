Milanesa

Messi's N1 food

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • 2 Eggs
  • Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. The first thing we are going to do is beat the eggs and add all the marinade ingredients inside. Let's mix everything well.
  2. Next: We are going to salt the Milanese and place them in the marinade, they have to be well soaked. We are going to cover them with plastic wrap and put them in the refrigerator for at least an hour. The more the better.
  3. After that time, we are going to take them out of the refrigerator. Then, Milanese by Milanese, we are going to bread them with the breadcrumbs, pressing on both sides until they are well coated.
  4. Finally, what remains is to fry them in very hot oil until they are golden brown and enjoy them very much.
